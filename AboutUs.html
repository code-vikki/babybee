The Baby Bee Story

To bee or not to Bee

Welcome to BabyBee!, your number one source for all kids clothing things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2021 by Vikkram and Priya, Baby Bee has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When the couple first started out,their passion for fashion drove them to quit their day jobs and turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online clothing store. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the fashion industry.
